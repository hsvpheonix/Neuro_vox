About NeuroVox

At NeuroVox, our mission is to empower individuals with speech impairments to communicate naturally and securely. We envision a world where everyone has a voice, regardless of their physical limitations. Our non-invasive brain-signal translation headband is designed to bridge the communication gap, offering a seamless and intuitive way to express thoughts and emotions.

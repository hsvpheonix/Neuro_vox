About NeuroVox

NeuroVox is a full-stack web application designed to help individuals with speech impairments communicate naturally and securely using a non-invasive brain-signal translation headband. It combines real-time brain state monitoring, emergency response, assistive technologies, and emotional expression—all wrapped in a sleek, accessible interface.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower individuals with speech impairments by transforming silence into hope through innovative neurotechnology. We envision a world where communication barriers are eliminated, enabling everyone to express themselves freely and securely.
